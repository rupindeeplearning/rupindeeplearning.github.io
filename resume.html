My Resume

 

I have a BE in Electronics Engineering from the University of Mumbai, India, an MSc in Medical Imaging from the University of Surrey, U.K. and an MASc in Electrical Engineering & Computer Science from the University of British Columbia, Canada

I have woked as a signal processing engineer at a Brain Imaging startup in Toronto called Cerebral Diagnostics Canada Inc.
I have then researched better algorithms for treating atrial fibrillation at Toronto General Hospital's Cardiology Department. My work there has resulted in several publications and a couple of patents. A brief list of publications is below.

I currently work as a risk analyst at the Bank of Montreal.
