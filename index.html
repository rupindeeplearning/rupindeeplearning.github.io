Welcome to my site

 

I am an electrical engineer by training and currently work as a Risk Analyst at a major bank in Canada. I have made this website because I am interested in Machine Learning (especially Deep Learning) both as a hobby and as the basis of a career and I am hoping to use this site as a showcase of my projects. This site is pretty raw at present but I hope to expand it and polish it fairly soon.


Most non-trivial projects I have done so far in Signal Processing or Machine/Deep Learning are private, since they are done for either my current employer or my previous one (a cardiac researcher at a major Toronto hospital). The projects on this site are mostly done by way of self-education.


The projects on this site are ones I have used to teach myself some new aspect of the ML ecosystem (my first look at PyTorch/FastAI, BERT etc.) or some skills that may come in handy for model deployment (simple projects to teach myself Streamlit, Flask, etc.).


My resume and current projects are both linked in the tabs at the top. Please do feel free to have a look around.
